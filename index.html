The Wright Brothers
biography, adventure
Synopsis

In this compelling biography, Pulitzer Prize-winning author David McCullough brings to life the extraordinary story of Wilbur and Orville Wright, two self-taught engineers who changed the world with their invention of the airplane. From their humble beginnings in a bicycle shop in Dayton, Ohio, to their groundbreaking first flight at Kitty Hawk in 1903, this book delves into their struggles, perseverance, and ultimate triumph. With meticulous research and vivid storytelling, McCullough captures the brothers’ unwavering determination, their setbacks, and the global impact of their achievements. The Wright Brothers is an inspiring tale of innovation, resilience, and the pursuit of an impossible dream.
